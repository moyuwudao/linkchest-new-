## 1. Overview

At LinkChest, we want you to be satisfied with your subscription. This Refund & Auto-Renewal Policy explains the conditions under which you may request a refund, and how to disable auto-renewal.

## 2. Refund Eligibility

You may be eligible for a refund under the following circumstances:

- **Within 7 days of purchase**: If you are not satisfied with your subscription, you may request a full refund within 7 days of the initial purchase or upgrade (**only applicable to purchases made through Alipay in the LinkChest APP, and where paid features have not been materially used**).
- **Service unavailability**: If our technical issues result in continuous service unavailability for more than 24 hours, you may request a prorated refund for the affected period.
- **Billing errors**: If you were incorrectly or duplicate-charged, we will issue a full refund for the erroneous amount.

## 3. Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds will not be issued in the following cases:

- Requests submitted more than 7 days after the purchase date
- Account suspended due to violation of Terms of Service
- Downgrading from a higher-tier plan to a lower-tier plan
- Unused time or features within the subscription period
- Subscriptions made through third-party channels (Apple App Store / Google Play / WeChat); contact the corresponding channel customer service for refunds

## 4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us through:

- **In-App**: LinkChest APP → Settings → Help & Feedback → Contact Us
- **Email**: support@linkchest.net

Please provide your account email, order number, and reason for refund. We will process your request within 5 business days.

## 5. Refund Methods

Refunds will be returned to the original payment method. Processing times vary:

- **Alipay**: 1-3 business days
- **WeChat Pay**: 1-7 business days
- **Credit/Debit Card**: 5-10 business days

## 6. Auto-Renewal Service Statement

### 6.1 Auto-Renewal Rules

If you choose "Enable Auto-Renewal" when subscribing, your subscription will automatically renew for the next billing cycle 24 hours before the expiration date. We will charge through your bound payment method (Alipay / WeChat).

- **First charge amount**: Equal to the listed price of your selected plan
- **Subsequent charge amount**: Equal to the listed price at renewal time (we will notify you in advance of any price changes)
- **Charge time**: 24 hours before the expiration of the current subscription cycle

### 6.2 How to Disable Auto-Renewal (Important)

**The APP does not provide a management entry. You can cancel auto-renewal through either of the following methods:**

**Via Alipay:**
1. Open the Alipay APP
2. Go to "Me" → "Settings" → "Payment Settings" → "Password-Free Payment / Auto-Deduction"
3. Find the "LinkChest (链藏)" service
4. Tap "Disable Service"

**Via WeChat (if using WeChat Pay):**
1. Open WeChat
2. Go to "Me" → "Services" → "Wallet" → "Payment Settings" → "Password-Free Payment / Auto-Renewal"
3. Find "LinkChest (链藏)"
4. Disable the service

### 6.3 Benefits After Disabling

- After disabling auto-renewal, **membership benefits within the current cycle remain until expiration**
- After expiration, auto-renewal stops and membership benefits expire
- Disabling the service **does not entitle you to a refund of already-charged fees** (if compliant with Section 2, refunds will follow the refund rules)

## 7. Subscription Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle. You may continue to use paid features until the end of the cycle.

## 8. Policy Changes

We reserve the right to modify this policy at any time. Changes take effect immediately upon publication on the website and in the APP. Material changes will be re-confirmed through an in-app dialog.
