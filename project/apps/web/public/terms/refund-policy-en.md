# Refund Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

## 1. Overview

At LinkChest, we want you to be satisfied with your subscription. This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which you may request a refund for your paid subscription.

## 2. Eligibility for Refunds

You may be eligible for a refund under the following circumstances:

- **Within 14 days of purchase**: If you are not satisfied with your subscription, you may request a full refund within 14 days of your initial purchase or upgrade.
- **Service unavailability**: If our service is unavailable for more than 24 consecutive hours due to technical issues on our end, you may request a prorated refund for the affected period.
- **Billing errors**: If you were incorrectly charged or double-charged, we will issue a full refund for the erroneous amount.

## 3. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be granted in the following cases:

- Requests made after 14 days from the purchase date
- Violations of our Terms of Service resulting in account suspension
- Downgrade from a higher-tier plan to a lower-tier plan
- Unused time or features during the subscription period

## 4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us at:

- Email: support@linkchest.net

Please include your account email and the reason for your refund request. We will process your request within 5 business days.

## 5. Refund Method

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase. Processing time may vary depending on your payment provider:

- Credit/Debit cards: 5-10 business days
- Other payment methods: Up to 15 business days

## 6. Subscription Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation will take effect at the end of your current billing period. You will continue to have access to your paid features until the end of that period.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to our website.

## 8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Refund Policy, please contact us at support@linkchest.net.
